About this app

This app allows you to keep track of the cards you have in your collection and the cards you are looking for.
Although I leave it here for anyone to use, this app was created for personal use. I don't mind about edition or language of the cards, so I am not going to add these details.
This app uses the same database that my other app IPGTree, if you use both apps you don't have to worry about having duplicate database
