IPGTree is an android app to help judging Magic the Gathering tournaments

This app includes

  • Documents: CR, JAR, AIPG, AMTR (yes, the annotated versions), disqualification process, banned lists, HJ announcement, links to useful resources.
  • Offline card database (you can search by name or by set and number).
  • Tree: The app make you questions about the problem you have and then gives you the solution.
  • Quiz: A quiz with questions about IPG and MTR to practice.
  • Decklist counter.
  • Draft timer (normal, 2HG and Rochester).
  • Round timer (you can time a call by pressing in the remaining time).
  • Life counter.
  • Supported languages: English and Spanish.

FAQs

Q: Why is the app not in the store?
A: I don't like the policies of the store.
Q: Will there be an iOS version?
A: No.
Q: No card appears in the Oracle.
A: Go to Settings and download the database.
Q: Can I change the number of sides of the dice?
A: Yes, press and hold the dice button.
Q: Can I change the descending counter to an ascending counter?
A: The counter goes to negative numbers, set the time to 0 and you will have an "ascending" counter.
Q: I messed up with a link in Advanced Settings
A: Delete all text of the link, a hint with the original link will appear, press and hold on it.
Q: What is that FTP server option in Advanced Settings?
A: The app can send the information of the life counter in real time to a computer so that the OBS automatically reads it when streaming a tabletop game (I do not offer it for free, so it is blocked by default, my contact info is below if you are interested).
